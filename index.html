FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How do I rename multiple files at once on Android?

Download the free Bulk Rename app, select an operation like Add Prefix or Serial Number, add your files using the built-in file browser, configure your options, preview the changes, and tap Apply. All your files will be renamed instantly.

Is Bulk Rename app really free?

Yes, Bulk Rename is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no hidden costs. All 10 rename operations and features are available for free, forever.

Can I undo file renames?

Yes! Bulk Rename keeps a complete history of all rename operations. You can view your history and undo any rename operation with a single tap to restore the original file names.

What file types can I rename?

Bulk Rename works with all file types including photos (JPG, PNG), videos (MP4, MOV), documents (PDF, DOC), music files (MP3, WAV), and any other file on your Android device.

Does the app require internet connection?

No, Bulk Rename works completely offline. All file processing happens locally on your device. We don't upload or access your files in any way.

What Android version is required?

Bulk Rename requires Android 7.0 (Nougat) or later. It works on phones and tablets from all manufacturers including Samsung, Google Pixel, OnePlus, Xiaomi, and more.
